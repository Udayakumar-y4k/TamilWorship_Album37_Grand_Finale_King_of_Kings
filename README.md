# Album 37 – Grand Finale: King of Kings

This Grand Finale album is a majestic declaration that Jesus Christ is the ultimate King above all history, empires, and powers. From the fall of great kingdoms to the eternal throne, every song leads to one truth: Jesus reigns forever. It serves as both a powerful conclusion to the Psalm series and a triumphant worship experience for the remnant church.

## Prayer

English:
Lord Jesus, King of Kings and Lord of Lords, we exalt You above every throne and every name. All kingdoms rise and fall, but Your Kingdom is everlasting. Help us live with surrendered hearts, declaring Your supremacy until You return in glory. Amen. Hallelujah!
